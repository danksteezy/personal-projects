Services

HD Video Production Services is a video production company specializing in on-line videos and commercials for personal and business web pages, Facebook Fan pages and YouTube. We also create music videos, corporate videos, promotional videos and documentaries.


We shoot our video productions on high definition. We offer exceptional quality and reasonable rates.

HD Video Production Services began operation in Vancouver in 2008

The company moved its main operation to Calgary in 2011 and has been providing quality video production services to Southern Alberta businesses, individuals and corporations ever since.

We like to help our clients understand the power of video and what it can do for their business the role it can play in bringing clients to their website and to their doors.

We tell our clients that video grabs attention like nothing else. While people generally won’t read everything you’ve written on a web page, they are very likely to click "play" on a video if there’s one on the page.
